Dan's Delicious Deviled Eggs

Time: 45 Minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 dozen eggs
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbsp minced onion or shallot
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Paprika

DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil the eggs.
  2. Peel the eggs.
  3. Cut the eggs in half long ways, scoop out the yolks into a separate bowl and organize the egg whites on a serving tray.
  4. Using a fork, mash up the yolks and add mustard, mayonnaise, onion, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper.
  5. Spoon the egg yolk mix into the egg white halves.
  6. Sprinkle with paprika.
  7. Don't get fat!
